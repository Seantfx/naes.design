About me

Sean Tiffonnet Product designer, design leader & problem solver

Bio

I'm a 32-year-old Franco-Irish designer based in Andalusia, Spain. Designing remotely for Zeliq and Nodale, bringing 10+ years of product thinking, design systems, and user-centered craft to every project.

I started designing at 14, teaching myself Photoshop to customize Counter-Strike visuals. What began as a hobby quickly turned into a business. At 16, I co-founded Shark-Config, a digital agency serving 100+ clients across e-commerce, restaurants, e-sport teams, and entrepreneurs.

After graduating from Epitech Digital in 2016, I joined 360Learning as the second designer and helped grow the design team to 8 members over nearly five years.

In 2021, I became the founding designer and first employee at Claap.io, building the entire product and brand from scratch. Claap earned Top 10 best-designed products of 2022 on ProductHunt, Product of the Day in 2022 and 2023, and was featured among the best screen recording apps in 2024. The company was later acquired by Lemlist.
